\begin{lemma}
If \((F,G)\) is \(H_s\)-free, then the product digraph \(D(F,G)\) is \(T_s\)-free.
\end{lemma}
\begin{proof}
SKETCH:
A transitive tournament in \(D(F,G)\), ordered by its tournament order, consists of product vertices \((a_i,b_i)\). Each product vertex gives \(a_i b_i\in E(F)\), and each forward tournament arc gives \(a_i b_j\in E(G)\) for \(i<j\). These data are exactly an \(H_s\) configuration in \((F,G)\), contradicting the hypothesis.
\end{proof}
