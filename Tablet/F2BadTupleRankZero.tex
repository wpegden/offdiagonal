\begin{helper}
For every \(2k\)-tuple
\[
  ab=(a_1,b_1,\ldots,a_k,b_k)\in(\mathbb F_2^p)^{2k},
\]
the initial rank from \(\noderef{F2BadTupleRank}\) is
\[
  r_0=0.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
By \(\noderef{F2BadTupleRank}\), \(r_0\) is the dimension of the span of the
\(a\)-vectors with index \(j<0\). There are no such indices, so this is the span
of the empty set, namely the zero subspace. The zero subspace has dimension
\(0\), hence \(r_0=0\).
\end{proof}
