\begin{helper}
For every \(2k\)-tuple
\[
  ab=(a_1,b_1,\ldots,a_k,b_k)\in(\mathbb F_2^p)^{2k}
\]
and every index parameter \(i\), the rank quantity \(r_i\) from
\(\noderef{F2BadTupleRank}\) satisfies
\[
  r_i\le p.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
By \(\noderef{F2BadTupleRank}\), \(r_i\) is the dimension of a subspace of
\(\mathbb F_2^p\), namely the span of some subset of the \(a\)-coordinates. The
dimension of any subspace is at most the dimension of its ambient vector space.
The ambient space \(\mathbb F_2^p\) has dimension \(p\), so \(r_i\le p\).
\end{proof}
