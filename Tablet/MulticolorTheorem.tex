\begin{theorem}
For every fixed \(\ell\ge 3\),
\[
  r(s;\ell)=\Omega(2^{(\ell-1)s/2}).
\]
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
SKETCH:
By \noderef{RandomHomomorphismColoringBound}, for
\[
  n=2^{(s/2-4)(\ell-1)}
\]
there is an \(\ell\)-edge-coloring of \(K_n\) with no monochromatic \(K_s\). Therefore \(r(s;\ell)>n\). Since \(\ell\) is fixed, the factor \(2^{-4(\ell-1)}\) is absorbed into the implicit positive constant in \(\Omega(2^{(\ell-1)s/2})\).
\end{proof}
