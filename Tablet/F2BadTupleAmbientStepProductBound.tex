\begin{helper}
For every \(2k\)-tuple
\[
  ab=(a_1,b_1,\ldots,a_k,b_k)\in(\mathbb F_2^p)^{2k}
\]
and every prefix length \(i\), let \(r_i\) be the rank quantity from
\(\noderef{F2BadTupleRank}\). Then
\[
  |\mathbb F_2^p|\cdot
  \left|\{\,y\in\mathbb F_2^p:\langle a_j,y\rangle=1
      \text{ for every }j<i\,\}\right|
  \le
  2^p\,2^{p-r_i}.
\]
\end{helper}
\begin{proof}
The ambient vector space \(\mathbb F_2^p\) has \(2^p\) elements, since each of
the \(p\) coordinates has two choices. By
\(\noderef{F2BadTuplePrefixFiberBound}\), the set of vectors \(y\) satisfying
the displayed dot-product equations has cardinality at most \(2^{p-r_i}\).
Multiplying these two bounds gives
\[
  |\mathbb F_2^p|\cdot
  \left|\{\,y:\langle a_j,y\rangle=1\text{ for every }j<i\,\}\right|
  \le 2^p\,2^{p-r_i}.
\]
\end{proof}
