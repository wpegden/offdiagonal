\begin{definition}
Let
\[
  ab=(a_1,b_1,\ldots,a_k,b_k)\in(\mathbb F_2^p)^{2k}.
\]
For a natural number \(i\), define the rank of the first \(i\) \(a\)-vectors by
\[
  r_i=\dim_{\mathbb F_2}\operatorname{span}\{a_j:1\le j\le k,\ j<i\}.
\]
This is the rank-sequence quantity used to count bad tuples; for \(i\le k\) it is
the dimension of the span of the \(a\)-vectors whose indices occur before \(i\).
\end{definition}
