\begin{definition}
Let
\[
  ab=(a_1,b_1,\ldots,a_k,b_k)\in(\mathbb F_2^p)^{2k}.
\]
The tablet uses zero-based Lean indices for the tuple entries: the Lean index
\(\ell<k\) names the paper vector \(a_{\ell+1}\). For a natural number \(i\),
define
\[
  r_i=\dim_{\mathbb F_2}
    \operatorname{span}\{a_{\ell+1}:0\le \ell<k,\ \ell<i\}.
\]
Thus \(r_0\) is the dimension of the empty span, and for \(1\le i\le k\) this is
exactly the paper's rank
\[
  \dim_{\mathbb F_2}\operatorname{span}\{a_1,\ldots,a_i\}.
\]
The sequence \((r_0,r_1,\ldots,r_k)\) is the rank sequence used in the
bad-tuple count.
\end{definition}
